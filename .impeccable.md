## Design Context

### Users
Temple administrators and campaign managers use this interface to monitor donation momentum, review donor activity, and update live campaign content from a desktop-first admin workspace.

### Brand Personality
Warm, trustworthy, devotional. The interface should feel polished, calm, and credible rather than flashy or overly corporate.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use a refined light dashboard with saffron, sandalwood, and deep temple-brown tones. The admin experience should feel premium and clean, with strong structure, soft gradients, and data panels that stay readable under heavy use.

### Design Principles
- Prioritize clarity over decoration so key donation information is easy to scan.
- Keep the visual language warm and brand-aligned with temple fundraising context.
- Make dense admin tasks feel organized through clear grouping, spacing, and section hierarchy.
- Preserve full editing capability while presenting the page as a professional dashboard first.
